ASIMO (Honda)

ASIMO, developed by Honda Motor Co., Ltd., is a humanoid robot first introduced in 2000. ASIMO (Advanced Step in Innovative Mobility) was designed for mobility, interaction, and service tasks, showcasing Hondaâ€™s expertise in robotics and AI.

ASIMO can walk, run, climb stairs, recognize gestures, and respond to voice commands. The robot integrates sensors, control algorithms, and artificial intelligence to operate autonomously in human environments. ASIMO serves as a research platform for robotics, inspiring advancements in humanoid mobility, safety, and human-robot interaction.
