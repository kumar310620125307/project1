KUKA Robotics

KUKA AG, founded in 1898 in Germany, is a pioneer in robotics and automation. The company provides robotic systems for industrial manufacturing, automotive, electronics, and logistics. KUKA is famous for its articulated robotic arms that perform welding, assembly, palletizing, and material handling with high precision.

KUKA emphasizes Industry 4.0 integration through its KUKA Connect platform, which provides real-time monitoring, predictive maintenance, and digital twins. The company has developed collaborative robots, known as LBR iiwa, which allow safe interaction between humans and robots on production floors.
